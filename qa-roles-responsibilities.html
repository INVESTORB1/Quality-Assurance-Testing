QA Roles & Responsibilities

Quality Assurance (QA) professionals play a vital role in the software development lifecycle. Their responsibilities include:

  • Planning and designing test strategies and test cases
  • Executing tests and reporting defects
  • Collaborating with developers and stakeholders to resolve issues
  • Ensuring that software meets quality standards and requirements
  • Maintaining documentation and test artifacts
  • Participating in process improvement initiatives

QA roles may include QA Analyst, Test Engineer, Automation Engineer, Performance Tester, and QA Lead, each with specific responsibilities tailored to the project and organization.
